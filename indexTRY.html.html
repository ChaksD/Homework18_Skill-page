About me

I believe in creating a more humane and empathetic world.

Prior to my UX/UI journey, I was an Owners Corporation Manager for over a decade. I felt increasingly unfulfilled and was craving a creative career where I could realize more ambitious ideas.

My “aha moment” was when I was dealing with a web development company to develop an App for a building I managed. When I learned about the role of UX/UI designer, I knew it was something I had to pursue.

The fundamentals of the two fields are the same, from constantly looking out for the needs of clients to possessing a desire to solve complex issues by coming up with new and innovative solutions.

In my free time, I am frequently seen making mixed media sculpture and paintings.
